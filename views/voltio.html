+ Performance

Engineered for a seamless gameplay, the Lumio 64 has the ability to offer classic games a smooth, delay-free performance. Optimized to handle many retro titles, its efficient processing ensures every game runs with no slowdowns.

GPU: Dual-Core G31 MP2

Fueled by the powerful dual-core G31 MP2 graphics processor, the Voltio Classic ensures smooth, sharp visuals with a responsive gameplay, making all games it run efficient and with minimal lag.

RAM: LPDDR4 1GB

With 1GB of LPDDR4 memory, the Voltio Classic handles quick save and load times, with smooth transitions, making every game experience seamless and fluid.

ROM: 64GB TF/Micro SD

Built with a 64GB of expandable storage with TF/Micro SD, there is ample room for your favourite games collection, making it possible to carry many titles wherever you go.

Battery: 3100mAh

With 31000mAh, the Voltio Classic has one of the longest lasting battery life as a retro pocket, offering up to 8 lasting hours on a full charge, ensuring less frequent recharges.
