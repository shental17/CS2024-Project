Our Origin Story

+

2019

The Spark of Nostalgia

It all started with a love for classic games. As we saw the growing appreciation for retro gaming, we realized there was an opportunity to blend our nostalgia with modern-day portability. The idea of Pyxels was born—bringing beloved retro experiences to a device that fits right in your pocket.

+

2020

First Concepts and Sketches

Armed with inspiration, we started sketching ideas for pocket-sized retro devices. The goal was to create something playful yet timeless, with a characterful logo and design that would appeal to both seasoned gamers and new enthusiasts. This is when 'Pyx' took shape, complete with the heart-centered 'P' to symbolize our love for gaming.

+

2021

Prototype Phase Begins

With the initial designs set, we moved into prototyping. This phase was all about perfecting the feel, display, and controls to ensure an authentic retro experience. We wanted Pyxels to feel as immersive as those hours spent in front of old-school consoles and arcades.

+

2022

Community Connections

As our prototypes came to life, we reached out to the retro gaming community, sharing early concepts and gathering feedback from passionate YouTubers and fans. Their input was invaluable, guiding us to refine the experience and making Pyxels feel like a true collaborative project.

+

2023

Official Launch of Pyxels

After countless hours of design, feedback, and fine-tuning, Pyxels officially launched. Our first wave of devices was met with excitement from retro enthusiasts and collectors alike. The heart in our 'Pyxels' logo had found a place in the community, and Pyxels began its journey as a brand committed to reviving classic gaming with a modern twist.

+

2024

Expanding the Universe

With our community’s continued support, Pyxels is only getting started. We’re working on new devices, collaborating with more creators, and dreaming up ways to make retro gaming even more accessible and enjoyable. The journey continues as we explore the endless possibilities of play and nostalgia.

Meet Pyx +

Say hello to Pyx—the playful, pixel-loving monster who’s here to keep the retro spirit alive! Pyx is more than just a mascot; they’re the heart and soul of Pyxels, capturing everything we love about classic gaming: adventure, imagination, and a sense of fun that never grows old.

Pyx symbolizes our passion for connecting people through nostalgia and play. Whether they're exploring vintage consoles or showing off the latest pocket devices, Pyx reminds us that gaming is all about joy, curiosity, and a dash of mischief!

Our Mission & Values

At Pyxels, we’re on a mission to bring the joy of retro gaming to everyone, no matter where they are. We believe in crafting devices that capture the excitement of the classics while adding a fresh, modern twist. Our values—Revive, Relive, Replay—guide everything we do, from design to community building, as we aim to create memorable experiences for gamers of all ages.

Revive.

We breathe new life into the classics, blending nostalgia with innovation. By reviving retro games in pocket-sized devices, we make timeless gaming accessible and exciting, creating a bridge between the past and the present.

Relive.

Our devices allow you to relive those cherished gaming moments and memories that never fade. Every detail, from the feel of the controls to the look of the display, is crafted to recreate that authentic retro experience—taking you back to the golden days of gaming.

Replay.

Gaming is about endless possibilities, and we believe in creating products you’ll want to replay over and over. With each Pyxels device, we aim to deliver an experience that’s as addictive as the classics, inviting you to press start again and again.
