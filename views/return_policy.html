Return Policy

Return and Exchange Policy

Returns

If you're not fully satisfied with your retro pocket purchase, we offer a 14-day return window. To be eligible for a return, the product must be unused and in its original packaging with all accessories and manuals included. Simply contact us at support@pyxels.com or fill in the enquiry form to start the return process.

Exchanges

We accept exchanges for defective or damaged items within 14 days of purchase. The product must be in its original condition. Please contact us at support@pyxels.com or fill in the enquiry form to initiate the exchange process, and our team will assist you in selecting a replacement item.

Non-Returnable Items

Items that have been used, damaged by the customer, or are not in their original condition will not be eligible for return or exchange.

Return Shipping Costs

The customer is responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the product was defective or damaged upon arrival. We recommend using a trackable shipping service for returns to ensure safe delivery.

Refunds

Once we receive the returned item, it will be inspected. If it meets the return criteria, we will process a refund to the original payment method. Refunds may take 5-10 business days to reflect in your account, depending on your payment provider.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding returns or exchanges, please reach out to our customer service team at support@pyxels.com or fill in the enquiry form. We are happy to assist you and ensure your satisfaction with your Pyxels purchase.
